Pepperoni Pizza

Description

In this recipe we will be showing how to make a delicious pepperoni pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 package Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prepared pizza pans.
  3. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, 18 to 20 minutes. Rotate pizza pans between the top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
