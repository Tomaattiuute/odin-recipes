Pizza Dough

Description

In this recipe, we will make pizza dough that can be made for a base for your pizza or baked on its own to make a delicious snack!

Ingredients

  • 3 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 (.25 ounce) envelopes Pizza Crust Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ⅓ cups very warm water
  • ⅓ cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute
  3. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes. Add more flour as needed.
  5. Divide dough in half.
