Pizza Sauce

Description

In this recipe we will be preparing a homemade pizza sauce for those great pizza nights

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. In a bowl whisk together the tomato paste with the water
  2. In a separate bowl mix the pepper,garlic powder and oregano
  3. Now just add the sugar and salt, then mix both bowls and whisk together until smooth
